About Climate Awareness

This project aims to sensitize people about climate change and promoting solutions We encourage tree planting, recycling and sustainable living for a greener future

Our Story

We started with a simple idea that says change begins at home . In 2024 , a small team of passionate community members came together , committed to finding practical, local solutions to the global climate crisis. Since then, we have successfully launched 1000 tree-planting drives and educated hundreds of students

Our Vision

To create a cleaner, greener and sustainable future world where everyone takes action to protect the environment

Our Mission

We aim to educate communities about climate change, promote tree planting, and responsible waste management.

Our Motivation

we start this project because we realized that climate change is real and affects our health , food, and future. Everyone has a role to play in processing our planet

Beneficiaries

Communities , schools and future generations benefit from the knowledge and awareness created through this project

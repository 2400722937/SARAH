CAUSES OF CLIMATE CHANGE

Climate change is primarily caused by human activities that release large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. the burning of fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas for electricity, heat, and transporatation is the largest source of carbon dioxide emissions. These gases trap heat from the sun , causing global temperatures to rise and altering weather patterns worldwide.
